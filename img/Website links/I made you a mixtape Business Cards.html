About this design

Back to designs

The eighties produced some indelibly cool nostalgia as proved by artists like Jamie T, who pioneered the comeback of the humble cassette tape! The art of the teenage mixtape speaks to anyone born before 1980, and will appeal to anyone who ever painstakingly created the perfect tape.

About this design

Jonathan Howells is a senior designer and illustrator with over 20 years experience. He is the Creative Director of multi-disciplinary design office Dinnick & Howells, whose past clients include Levi Strauss, Glaxo Smith Kline and Lindt Chocolate. A self-confessed design geek, Jonathan runs a typography blog and is obsessed with endless possibilities of letterforms.

FAQ's

  • What are MOO Designs?
    MOO designs are packs of cards custom-designed by professional designers and artists. They’re perfect if you don’t have your own card design, of if you’re looking for something that’s tailored specifically to your industry. You can add your details and have us send you a pack of cards that are as professional as you are!
  • I don't like one of the cards
    No problem! You can remove any card from a pack when you start customising them. That way the pack truly represents exactly what you want them to.

    Alternatively, you can build your own fully customised Business Cards using our Uploader tool right here
  • Can I get these designs on your other products too?
    We plan on making complimentary designs for all our other products. However, it's a work in progress so please bear with us if you can't find every design for every product right now.
