Outreach, Link Building, and SEO Products for eCommerce companies

I’m an independent Digital Outreach, SEO, and Link Building consultant for eCommerce companies.

Unlike other SEO consultants, I’m focused on white-hat strategies to earn your business high-quality, relevant links and help you get found online through Digital Outreach. And I don’t bill by the hour which means you never have to watch the clock, pay for change requests, or risk blowing the budget.

Tell me more

Here are a few things that I can offer you:

  • Traffic Powerup, a monthly Digital Outreach and Link Building service. You already have a website. I work with lots of people to slowly, incrementally increase the traffic to their sites every month.
  • Digital Outreach Plan — Want something smaller? I can prepare a Digital Outreach Plan for your business, helping you build relationships with your dream partners, reach new audiences, and increase your sales.
  • Website X-Ray — Are you wondering why you aren’t ranking up there with your competitors? I can pick apart everything that’s wrong with your website and your SEO, providing you with an actionable report showing you clear next steps to grow your traffic.
  • One-Off Projects — I work on between four and six one-off consulting projects every year, and they range from Search Engine Optimization to Link Building to Outreach Consulting to Digital Public Relations.
