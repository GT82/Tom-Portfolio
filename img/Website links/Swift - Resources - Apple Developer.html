Xcode 6

Download Xcode 6 and add new Swift code or frameworks to your existing apps, or create a brand new app using 100 percent Swift code. With the release of Xcode 6.1 and iOS 8, Swift 1.1 is final, and you can build and submit your iOS apps written with Swift to the App Store today.
