"The #1 Most Effective

A personal note from the founder of List Goal, Bryan Harris

I learned a lesson a long time ago: if you want to be successful, find out what the most successful people are doing and focus on that...

... and all of the smart people online are focused on one thing.

Growing their email list.

The problem is this: Focus is incredibly hard.

Last year my friend Noah challenged me to focus on one single business goal.

I picked the number 75. I wanted 75 new email subscribers per day. So I wrote it on the whiteboard in my office. That was my single focus in life for the next 10 months.

That one number simplified my entire business. No longer did I have to wrestle with decisions. Any time an opportunity or tactic presented itself, I simply asked myself…

"Will this help me hit 75?"
If so, then I did it.
If it didn’t I ignored it.
The results?

10 months later my list had grown from 200 subscribers to 10,000 subscribers and I successfully launched my first product (full case study here).
